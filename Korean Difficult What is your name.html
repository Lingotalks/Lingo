[dahng-shin-eui ee-reum-eun moo-eo-sing-ga-yo?]

What is dangsinui ireumeun mueosinyo?

This is a formal way to ask someone's name in Korean.

Used in a sentence

"dangsinui ireumeun mueosinyo, Chingu?"
